How the score works. Every claim is graded by a Brier score against what actually happened: saying “here we go” (99%) on a deal that collapsed is punished hard; “in talks” (35%) on the same deal barely dents you. Scores are shrunk toward the average by sample size (K=20) so a spammer with a few lucky calls can’t top the table, and a small bonus rewards genuinely breaking a deal early.
